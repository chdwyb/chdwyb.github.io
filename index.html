Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor in the School of Information Engineering, Chang'an University, where I also received Ph.D. degree in 2026, supervised by Prof. Tao Gao (高涛). I worked as a visiting scholar at the School of Computing, Australian National University in 2023, where I conducted research with Senior Lecturer Jing Zhang (张静).

My research interests include computer vision, general image restoration, remote sensing image processing, unpaired learning, and multi-modal learning.
